Getting Started

Begin Your Cultivation

Your immortal journey begins with a single step. Follow the path below.

01

Join the Discord Server

Enter our cultivation community and accept the welcome ritual to register your cultivator profile. Your journey begins the moment you step through the gate.

/start
02

Begin Daily Cultivation

Meditate each day to gather Qi. Qi is the lifeblood of your progression — without it, breakthroughs are impossible. Complete daily missions for bonus Spirit Stones.

/cultivate /mission
03

Join or Found a Sect

Sects provide protection, shared resources, and collective power. A lone cultivator rarely survives the long road to immortality — find your people.

/sect join /sect create
04

Attempt a Breakthrough

When you have gathered enough Qi and obtained the required item, dare to shatter your realm ceiling. Success brings power; failure costs resources — but not your life.

/breakthrough
05

Ascend to Immortality

Seven realms stand between you and the peak. Each ascension changes your power fundamentally. The True Immortal title is held by only one cultivator at a time.

/leaderboard
